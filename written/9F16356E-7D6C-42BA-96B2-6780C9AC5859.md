# spheroid


## Noun

1. a shape that is generated by rotating an ellipse around one of its axes; “it looked like a sphere but on closer examination I saw it was really a spheroid”
	- Synonyms
		- ellipsoid of revolution
	- Less specific
		- round shape
	- Related
		- ellipsoid
		- ellipsoidal
		- spheroidal
