## Upload an image via the Snap.as API.

This shortcut uses [the Snap.as API](https://developers.snap.as/docs/api) to upload single images to Snap.as. At install, youâ€™ll be required to provide your Write.as/Snap.as API token, which you can acquire with [my Siri Shortcut](https://routinehub.co/shortcut/11589/) or [manually with the Write.as API](https://developers.write.as/docs/api/#authenticate-a-user).
